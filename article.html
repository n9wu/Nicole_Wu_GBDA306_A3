For all people who menstruate, you may notice that your fertile window shifts. Standard models often cite a 28-day cycle, but many people report significant personal variations.

🧠 What we know vs. What we're still learning
Confirmed

Hormonal shifts typically cause physical symptoms like energy changes.

Still Learning

How individual micro-stressors uniquely impact the timing of ovulation for different bodies.

A quick note for you: If you're feeling different this month, you're not alone. It's completely normal for your body to move at its own pace.
